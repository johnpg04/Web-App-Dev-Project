Frequently Asked Questions

UH Sugar Land offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs in fields such as business, education, and technology.

You can contact the admissions office by email at admissions@uh.edu or by phone at (713) 743-1010.

Students have access to libraries, career services, counseling, and state-of-the-art facilities to support their academic journey.

An undergraduate student should recognize that course work in college will require a significant amount of work outside the classroom - traditionally recommended at 2-3 hours for every hour in class.

The Student Health Center & Campus Pharmacy is located on-campus and offers non-emergent, outpatient medical care as well as psychiatry, orthopedic, immunization, laboratory, and pharmacy services to all currently enrolled UH students.
